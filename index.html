How Many Types of Print Drivers Are There?

There are two major types of print drivers:

  1. Cloud Driver
  2. Device Driver

Cloud Drivers: These allow printing without installing a driver on your computer. You only need an internet connection or to be on the same network.

Device Printer Drivers: These require ongoing communication between the printer hardware and your computer's operating system. They must be installed to ensure your Canon printer works properly.
